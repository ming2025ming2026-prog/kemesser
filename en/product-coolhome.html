PRODUCT FEATURES

Product Features

01

Ice-free Contamination Control

Samples avoid direct contact with wet ice, reducing water, label damage, displacement and contamination risks.

02

Consistent Cooling Rate

Each tube receives consistent cooling conditions for more uniform temperature control and standardized procedures.

03

Upright Organized Samples

Tubes remain upright with visible labels for easier identification, handling and workflow organization.

04

Reusable Design

Reusable cooling cores eliminate continuous wet-ice consumption and reduce routine cleanup.

05

Extended Temperature Control

Cooling cores provide sustained temperature control for processing, temporary storage and bench-to-bench transfer.

06

Modular Compatibility

Compatible BlockHome modules support multiple tube types, plates and sample containers.
